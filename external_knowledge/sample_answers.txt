SAMPLE ANSWER 2
Question Type: Tell me about a technical project
Answer:
One of my most important projects was my Master’s thesis on 3D group detection using distance-based clustering. The goal was to detect pedestrian groups in 3D scenes using LiDAR-based detections without relying on semantic cues or training-based methods. I designed a lightweight pipeline where pedestrian detections were processed and then grouped using DBSCAN based on geometric proximity. A key challenge was creating a method that remained simple but still useful in crowded or partially occluded scenes. This project taught me how to structure a computer vision pipeline, evaluate design choices, and explain technical results clearly.
